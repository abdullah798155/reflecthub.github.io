Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23-02-2025


1. YouTube Video Links

Our website displays links to YouTube videos. We do not host or embed YouTube videos directly. When you click on a video link, you will be redirected to YouTube, which is governed by YouTube’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track website usage, including visitor counts and geographic locations. This helps us understand how users interact with our site. Google Analytics collects data via cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing Google's Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. Google Forms

We use Google Forms to collect feedback and suggestions. Any information you submit is handled by Google and is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Clipboard & Sharing Features

Our website provides features for copying and sharing text content:

  • Clipboard Access: The "Copy" feature allows users to copy text to their clipboard. We do not read or access clipboard contents. No clipboard data is stored, transmitted, or used beyond the copy action.
  • Web Share API: The "Share" feature uses the Web Share API to allow users to share text content. This action is initiated by the user, and we do not track or store shared information.

Both features operate entirely within the user’s device and do not involve any server-side data collection.
